Pricing

We keep it simple and transparent: vendor wholesale + 15% platform margin (minimum fee $15).

What you pay

You pay the wholesale service cost set by the technician plus a small platform margin. No hidden fees.

What you get

Cancellation policy

Cancel ≥24h: full refund. Inside 24h: vendor late‑cancel fee (e.g., 25%) and refund the remainder.

No‑show (customer): minimum fee. No‑show (vendor): full refund to you.

How billing works

  1. Pick a slot and pay at booking (secure checkout).
  2. We confirm the technician and lock the slot.
  3. After completion, the technician is paid their wholesale; we retain the margin automatically.
